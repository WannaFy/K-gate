  • The four buttons at the top are the real music play buttons though their names may cause you to think they are fake, they can play real musics not just print the words in the cmd.

  • input the song name in the entry. click the '真正的音乐播放按钮', musics will be print untill every songs are played! BUT I recommend that you try to avoid use it, because every song will be played and you can do nothing but wait!

  • input name or artist or year or source to divide the songs in groups and print all the songs in the cmd!

  • click the button called"最近播放的歌曲" will print the recent songs(less than 100) you have listend.

  • if you want to add some songs to 'Gate k', plase input six arguments to add them. The six arguments are number(choose the number what ever you like), song name, the artist, year, lyrics, source and divide these factors by ','!

  • input the song name and click the buttton called"删除歌曲" to delete songs

  • input the song name and click the button called "搜索歌曲信息" to see the data of the song you want.
